Arctic Wolf AI layoff details

Reported layoffs: 250

Industry: Technology · Geography: Global

The Register reported cybersecurity vendor Arctic Wolf laid off 250 workers in a restructuring the company said would help it operate more efficiently and continue investing in its Superintelligence platform and agentic Security Operations Center.

While these decisions are difficult, they position Arctic Wolf to operate more efficiently, continue investing in our Superintelligence platform and Agentic SOC, and deliver strong value to customers.

The Register reported the layoffs represented less than 10% of Arctic Wolf's workforce and appeared to affect sales, product development, and marketing. Because the company spokesperson explicitly tied the restructuring to continued investment in its AI-oriented Superintelligence platform and Agentic SOC, this is classified as explicit AI cited.
