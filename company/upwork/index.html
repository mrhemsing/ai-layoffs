Upwork AI layoff details

Explicit AI citedprimary

Reported layoffs: 145

Industry: Technology · Geography: Global

Upwork announced a restructuring plan expected to reduce its workforce by approximately 24%, with CEO Hayden Brown telling employees the company had rethought every function around people and technology and that AI lets smaller product and engineering teams have more impact.

AI means smaller, differently resourced teams in product and engineering can make a bigger impact than ever.

Upwork's Form 8-K gives the official reduction as approximately 24% of the company's workforce. Final Round AI estimated the absolute impact at about 145 jobs out of a 600-person headcount; because the company source gives only a percentage, the count should be treated as a secondary estimate. This is classified as explicit AI cited because the CEO memo directly links the new team structure to AI-enabled smaller teams.
