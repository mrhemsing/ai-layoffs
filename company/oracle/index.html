Industry: Technology · Geography: United States

Reuters first reported Oracle was planning thousands of job cuts as AI data center costs surged, CNBC later confirmed Oracle had started telling employees about a latest layoff round, and MarketWatch reported the total cuts were estimated to be as many as 30,000 people.

The layoffs will impact divisions across Oracle and may be implemented as soon as this month... Some cuts will be aimed at job categories that the company expects will shrink due to AI.

Reuters attributed the initial report to Bloomberg and said Oracle declined comment; CNBC later confirmed with people familiar with the matter that Oracle had started telling employees about the cuts. MarketWatch reported the layoffs were estimated to be as many as 30,000 people, but also noted it was not clear whether all of those layoffs had already occurred or whether more were still coming later in the year, so layoffsCount remains null.
