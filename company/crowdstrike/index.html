CrowdStrike AI layoff details

Explicit AI citedprimary_source

Reported layoffs: 500

Industry: Cybersecurity · Geography: United States

CrowdStrike said it would cut about 500 jobs as AI-driven efficiencies flattened its hiring curve and reshaped how it operates.

AI flattens our hiring curve, and helps us innovate from idea to product faster. It streamlines go-to-market, improves customer outcomes, and drives efficiencies across both the front and back office.

CEO George Kurtz tied the 5% workforce cut directly to AI in a memo attached to the SEC filing; Reuters roundup and trade coverage echoed the linkage.
