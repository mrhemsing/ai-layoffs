Recruit Holdings cut about 1,300 jobs across Indeed and Glassdoor while telling staff it had to adapt to AI-driven change in hiring and product development.

AI is changing the world, and we must adapt by ensuring our product delivers truly great experiences for job seekers and employers.

Reuters-described internal memo and CBS coverage both connect the cuts to Recruit's AI-driven simplification of hiring and internal productivity work.
