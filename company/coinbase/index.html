Coinbase AI layoff details

Explicit AI citedreputable_reporting

Reported layoffs: 700

Industry: Finance · Geography: Global

CNBC and Reuters reported Coinbase will cut roughly 14% of its workforce, about 700 jobs, as CEO Brian Armstrong cited market volatility and AI quickly changing how the company operates.

We are adjusting early and deliberately to rebuild Coinbase to be lean, fast, and AI-native. We need to return to the speed and focus of our startup founding, with AI at our core.

CNBC quoted Armstrong saying AI is changing how Coinbase works and that the company is rebuilding to be lean, fast, and AI-native. Reuters also reported new AI tools allow non-technical teams to ship code and automate tasks that previously required larger headcounts. Market volatility and cost reduction are also cited drivers, so the entry is explicit-AI-cited but not framed as AI as the only cause.
