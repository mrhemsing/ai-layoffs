Walmart said Tuesday it would cut or relocate about 1,000 corporate workers as it looks to combine more of its global-technology and product teams.

The WSJ article says the review was led by Daniel Danker, Walmart's head of global AI acceleration, and Suresh Kumar, its head of global technology, and that they decided to streamline teams to operate more efficiently. This is included as AI-adjacent restructuring because the affected organization overlaps technology/product and AI acceleration leadership, but the available WSJ excerpt does not say AI automation directly replaced the workers.
